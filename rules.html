Server Rules

Keep it fair & friendly. Breaking rules can lead to mutes/bans.

  1. No cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs.
  2. No doxxing, harassment, hate speech, or threats.
  3. Respect staff decisions. Appeal in the proper channel.
  4. No advertising other servers without permission.
  5. Use common sense; if it feels scummy, don’t do it.

Edit rules.html in your repo to change these.
